As an Artist
the concrete jungle is his canvas, and graffiti is his language. As a hacker, parkour athlete, cyclist, and philosopher, Evederus's graffiti lifestyle is a vibrant fusion of creativity, rebellion, and exploration.

Graffiti as Rebellion:

For Evederus, graffiti is more than just art—it's a form of rebellion. Each tag and mural is a proclamation of defiance, a bold statement against the constraints of society and the forces of oppression. Through his graffiti, he challenges authority and empowers others to question the status quo.

The Art of Movement:

Evederus's passion for parkour infuses his graffiti with a sense of fluidity and motion. Just as he navigates the urban landscape with grace and agility, his graffiti flows seamlessly across walls and surfaces, reflecting the rhythm of his athletic prowess.

Exploring the Cityscape:

On his bike, Evederus explores the hidden corners and forgotten spaces of the city, seeking out new canvases for his art. From abandoned buildings to bustling alleyways, he sees the world through the eyes of an explorer, always on the lookout for inspiration and adventure.

Art should comfort the disturbed and disturb the comfortable.

Philosophy in Graffiti:

As a philosopher, Evederus contemplates the deeper meaning behind his art. Each tag and mural is a reflection of his thoughts and beliefs, a way to spark dialogue and challenge conventional wisdom. Through his graffiti, he seeks to provoke thought and inspire others to see the world in new ways.

For Evederus, graffiti is a way to leave his mark on the world—to make his voice heard in a society that often ignores the voices of the marginalized and oppressed. It's a way to reclaim public spaces and inject them with color, vibrancy, and life. It's a way to connect with others and build community through shared experiences and shared spaces.
